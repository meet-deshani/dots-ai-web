# Page: Pricing
**Route**: `/pricing`

## Content Strategy
**Pivot**: From "Cost" to "Investment".
**Headline**: Stop the Margin Leakage. Start the Acceleration.
**Sub-headline**: No complex SQL. No technical debt. Just analytical sense delivered via tools that pay for themselves in months, not years.

### The Logic
We treat pricing as a medical prescription.
1.  **Diagnosis**: The Wedge (Audit).
2.  **Procedure**: The Build/Sprint.
3.  **Health Maintenance**: The Retainer.

### Pricing Tiers

#### Tier 1: The Wedge (Strategic Growth Blueprint)
- **Goal**: Diagnosis of "invisible" manual inefficiencies.
- **Price**: $2,500 – $3,500.
- **Includes**: Data Hygiene Audit, Process Blueprint, Custom ROI Roadmap.
- **Target**: Low-risk entry to build trust.

#### Tier 2: The Sprint (5-Day Automation Sprint)
- **Goal**: Technical Validation / Functional Prototype.
- **Price**: $5,000.
- **Includes**: Secured Sandbox, Zero-Knowledge Interface, POC.
- **Promise**: Eliminate "Vaporware Fatigue".

#### Tier 3: The Build (Custom Analytics Transformation)
- **Goal**: Full Vertical Integration (Revenue Recovery).
- **Price**: Project-Based (starts $15k).
- **Includes**: Custom Dashboarding, Secured Data Pipeline (RAG/SLM), Training.

#### Tier 4: The Retainer (Managed AI Operations)
- **Goal**: Recurring stability and optimization.
- **Price**: $3,000 – $5,000 / month.
- **Includes**: Model Monitoring, Continuous Optimization, Priority Support.
- **Analogy**: "Your backend AI team".

### Key Elements
- **Price of Inaction (Choose Your Pain)**:
    - **D2C RTO**: Losing $3k/mo on returns. Payback in 4 months.
    - **Missed Leads**: Losing $45k/mo on missed calls. Payback on 1st lead.
- **Strict SOW Policy**: No "science projects".
- **Zero-Knowledge Interface**: Highlighted as a core feature of all tiers.
